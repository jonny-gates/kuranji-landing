About Us

We are Kuranji, a coastal clothing brand from the UK’s sunny South Coast. Our clothing combines our love of impeccable quality with laid-back coastal living; we are the slow movers with sky-high standards.

Think 100% original handmade prints by local artists, carefully cut shapes, quality stitching and sumptuous organic cotton. Great for the beach, great for going out, great for life.
